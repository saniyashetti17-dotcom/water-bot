# 💧 Water Safety Awareness Bot

A Dialogflow-style chatbot project for checking basic water safety conditions and providing health risk awareness, prevention tips, and report history.

## 📌 Project Description

Water Safety Awareness Bot is an intelligent chatbot designed to help users understand whether water appears safe or unsafe for basic usage. It collects details such as water source, color, smell, clarity, and pH value. Based on the information provided by the user, the bot gives a result like **Safe**, **Moderate Risk**, or **Unsafe**.

The bot also provides:
- health risk awareness
- purification suggestions
- water quality score
- report history
- dashboard insights

## 🎯 Objectives

- To develop a water safety awareness chatbot
- To collect water-related details from the user
- To identify whether water appears safe or unsafe
- To provide awareness about water-borne health risks
- To suggest prevention and purification tips
- To maintain report history and dashboard analytics

## ✨ Features

- Live chatbot UI
- Water source, color, smell, clarity, and pH based checking
- Water quality score
- Safe / Moderate / Unsafe result
- Purification suggestion box
- Health risk guidance
- Dashboard cards
- Report history
- CSV export
- Sample demo flow
