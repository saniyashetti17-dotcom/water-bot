Project Description

Water Safety Awareness Bot helps users check basic water safety through a conversation. It collects water source, color, smell, clarity and pH, then gives Safe, Moderate Risk or Unsafe result with score and purification suggestion.


Objectives

  • To create a water safety awareness chatbot.
  • To collect water quality details step-by-step.
  • To show safety result with water quality score.
  • To give disease risk and purification suggestions.
  • To maintain dashboard, history and report export.

Intent Flow Used in Bot

  • Default Welcome Intent — Greets user
  • water.check.start — Starts flow
  • water.check.source — Source
  • water.check.color — Color
  • water.check.smell — Smell
  • water.check.clarity — Clarity
  • water.check.ph_known — pH known?
  • water.check.ph_value — pH value
  • water.check.result — Result
  • water.risk.info — Risk info
  • water.safety.tips — Tips
  • water.emergency.help — Emergency help
  • water.restart — Restart
  • water.cancel — Cancel
  • Default Fallback Intent — Unknown input
