Project Description

Water Safety Awareness Bot is an intelligent chatbot designed to help users understand whether water appears safe or unsafe for basic usage. It collects details such as water source, color, smell, clarity, and pH value. Based on the information provided by the user, the bot gives a simple result like safe, moderate risk, or unsafe. It also provides health risk awareness and prevention tips related to unsafe water.

Objectives

  • To develop a water safety awareness chatbot using Dialogflow.
  • To collect water-related information from the user in a step-by-step manner.
  • To identify whether water appears safe or unsafe.
  • To provide awareness about water-borne health risks.
  • To suggest prevention and safe water usage tips.
  • To create a structured chatbot flow using intents, entities, and contexts.

Intent Flow Used in Bot

  • 1. Default Welcome Intent — Greets the user and introduces the bot.
  • 2. Default Fallback Intent — Handles unknown or unmatched input.
  • 3. water.check.start — Starts the water safety checking flow.
  • 4. water.check.source — Collects water source.
  • 5. water.check.color — Collects water color.
  • 6. water.check.smell — Collects water smell information.
  • 7. water.check.clarity — Collects clarity level.
  • 8. water.check.ph_known — Asks whether the user knows pH.
  • 9. water.check.ph_value — Accepts numeric pH value.
  • 10. water.check.result — Displays final water safety result.
  • 11. water.risk.info — Explains health risks of unsafe water.
  • 12. water.safety.tips — Gives safe water prevention tips.
  • 13. water.emergency.help — Gives urgent health guidance.
  • 14. water.restart — Restarts the water checking process.
  • 15. water.cancel — Stops the chatbot process.
