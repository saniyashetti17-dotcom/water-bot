Project Description

Water Safety Awareness Bot checks water safety using source, color, smell, clarity and pH. It shows live score, safety result, purification suggestion, disease risk and lab-style report.

Objectives

  • To develop a water safety awareness chatbot.
  • To collect water details step-by-step.
  • To show live prediction and score.
  • To provide purification and health risk guidance.
  • To maintain dashboard, report and history.

Intent Flow Used in Bot

  • Default Welcome Intent — Greets user
  • water.check.start — Starts flow
  • water.check.source — Source
  • water.check.color — Color
  • water.check.smell — Smell
  • water.check.clarity — Clarity
  • water.check.ph_known — pH known
  • water.check.ph_value — pH value
  • water.check.result — Result
  • water.risk.info — Risk info
  • water.safety.tips — Tips
  • water.emergency.help — Emergency
  • water.restart — Restart
  • water.cancel — Cancel
  • Default Fallback Intent — Unknown input
